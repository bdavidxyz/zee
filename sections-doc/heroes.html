Hero sections

A hero section is the first thing your user see when the website opens. Therefore, it is the most strategic section of the landing page.

hero1

Inspired from geskeleton, the centered headline and CTA can't be left unread. Three horizontal icons and text can underline the unfair advantanges.

 

hero2
